## Research Overview

This technical research focuses on identifying and evaluating **market data APIs** that provide the historical fundamental data required for the BetterInvesting Stock Selection Guide (SSG), with a specific emphasis on **international coverage** (Switzerland, Germany, France).

### Proposed Research Scope

1. **Provider Evaluation**: Benchmarking AlphaVantage, EOD Historical Data, Yahoo Finance, and others.
2. **Data Coverage Integrity**: Verifying 10-year availability for non-US tickers.
3. **Integration Patterns**: REST/Websocket protocols, rate limits, and authentication strategies.
4. **Cost/Benefit Analysis**: Comparing free vs. paid tiers for hobbyist vs. power-user scenarios.
